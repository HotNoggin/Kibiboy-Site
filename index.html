Your teeny tool to make arcade-y games!

The KIBIBOY is a free, open-source fantasy console. It's built with its own specs and limitations, just like a physical console, but runs on your device. Your KIBIBOY comes with a command line and a cart editor for creating and playing arcade-y games.
